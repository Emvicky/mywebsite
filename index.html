about us

Dawagold Media, is a subsidiary of Dawagold Logistics Nigeria Limited.

Dawagold Media is a fast growing company that basically focuses on media printing, Exhibition, corporate branding, event management and logistics, direction signage. We are equipped to take over as the lead Media and Exhibition industry with great ideas and skills.
